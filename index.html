About the Founder.

Founder of the Mekuba Group of Companies.


B. Shivaraman

Born in Nemmara village, Palakkad district in Kerala, Shivaraman was an Industrialist in post-independent India. A chemical engineer from Indian Institute of Science [IISc] Bangalore, he has had an illustrious career at several top companies including Tata Chemicals Gujarat, Burma Shell and Indian Oil Refineries Limited, a Govt. of India enterprise.

He was selected to lead a team to the Eastern European and British Oil refineries, after which he took over as Chief Process and Production Engineer at IOCL Noonmati Refinery at Guwahati, Assam.After shifting base to the South in the early 70’s, he became a consultant to Madras Refineries Ltd [now part of Chennai Petroleum Corporation Limited] before starting Mekuba Petro Products in 1974.

Mr. Shivaraman was awarded the Vijay Shree Award, presented by the International Friendship Society of India (IFSI), for enriching human life and outstanding achievements.His values and principles continue to guide the second and third generations of the company to this day.
