M-SHIELD

LACQUER COATING FOR ALUMINIUM BOARDS

M-SHEILD is a solvent-based single-pack excellent anti-corrosive protective clear coating for ferrous and nonferrous metal surfaces.

FEATURES:

-Easy to apply and fast drying
-Excellent Bonding with Aluminum
-Good Alkaline Resistance
-Compatible with Shuttering oil after drying

Properties

  • Apperance - Clear and Transparent
  • Coverage - 14-16 sqmt/ltr
  • DFT - 25-35 microns
  • Touch to dry - 15-20mis
  • Hard dry - 8 Hours
